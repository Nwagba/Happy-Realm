About Us

Happy Realm is a Bible club dedicated to teaching children and teenagers the good news of Jesus Christ in a fun, engaging, and meaningful way.

Founded in 2021, we've been helping young people develop a strong spiritual foundation through interactive lessons, activities, and community building.

Our mission is to create a safe space where kids can explore their faith, ask questions, and grow in their relationship with God.

Our Founder

Pastor Success Nwagba

Pastor Success founded Happy Realm in 2021 with a vision to create a space where children could encounter God in a real and personal way.

With over 10 years of experience in children's ministry, he brings passion, knowledge, and creativity to every lesson.

"Seeing children develop a personal relationship with Jesus is the greatest joy of my life," says Pastor Success.
